The Expanded Peplum Jacket Series

Inspired by the sculptural elegance of the peplum jacket silhouette, this series by Kenneth J. Lewis Sr. explores fashion as architecture—translating tailored form, movement, and structure into expressive painterly compositions. Originals are available to collect, and a limited edition print offering is available for collectors who want the series in archival form.

Collect the Originals

Each work is an original oil painting that begins with a sketch and acrylic wash and is finished with loose, expressive brushwork. The Peplum Jacket Series is designed to be collected individually or as a cohesive set.

Originals: $1,800 each Oil on canvas • 24 x 30 inches
