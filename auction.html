Private & Confidential Bidding

1) The Great Migration

Medium: Acrylic on archival paper

Frame: Black contemporary frame

Size: 28 × 32 inches

Starting Bid: $500

A powerful monochromatic composition capturing a historic moment of resilience and transition. The expressive brushwork and stark contrast bring to life a family portrait that speaks to the courage and endurance of those who journeyed northward seeking opportunity and hope during the Great Migration. The textured orange-gold background radiates warmth, while the silhouetted figures evoke both dignity and memory.

2) Basquiat Tri-Fold (Side A & Side B)

Medium: Mixed media on linen panel

Size: 70 × 48 inches (tri-fold)

Starting Bid: $700

A vibrant, two-sided folding panel inspired by the raw energy of street art and the visual language of Jean-Michel Basquiat.

Side A bursts with chromatic intensity—layered skull motifs, graffiti-style text, and abstract symbols create a rhythm of chaos and commentary on culture and identity.

Side B features skeletal musicians and crowned figures, blending humor and critique in a spirited conversation about creativity, struggle, and truth. Both sides pulse with electric color and emotional urgency, transforming the tri-fold into a standing dialogue between life, art, and mortality.

3) Ephemeral Glow

Medium: Acrylic on presentation panel

Frame: Traditional black frame

Image Size: 26 × 32 inches

Frame Size: 30 × 40 inches

Starting Bid: $600

A haunting yet luminous still life of an hourglass and candle rendered in molten oranges and deep shadows. The work symbolizes the fleeting nature of time and the burning persistence of spirit. The textured strokes capture the interplay between light and decay—a meditation on mortality and the quiet beauty of moments that fade yet continue to glow in memory.

4) Pencil Point People #10

Medium: Mixed media on canvas

Size: 30 × 40 inches

Starting Bid: $650

From the “Pencil Point People” series, this piece reimagines community and individuality through stylized elongated forms dressed in vivid patterns and geometric precision. The group stands confidently against an architectural backdrop, blending rhythm, fashion, and posture into a celebration of self-expression. Each figure’s shape and color reflect a distinct story—together forming a collective narrative of unity and identity within diversity.
