Kenneth J. Lewis Sr.

Kenneth J. Lewis Sr. is a self-taught contemporary artist based in New Jersey. Starting his painting journey at the age of 47, Kenneth has developed a distinctive style that blends abstract expressionism with geometric landscapes and vibrant colors. His works often explore the interplay between structure and spontaneity, inviting viewers to dive into a meditative experience through form and hue.

With a background rooted in creativity and athleticism, Kenneth brings high energy and a stress-free approach to his art-making process. Over the years, he has showcased his work in multiple solo and group exhibitions, and his paintings can be found in both public and private collections.

Kenneth also shares his passion through the KJL Podcast Channel, where he hosts art history segments, interviews fellow artists, and leads engaging paint parties and workshops. Through his creative endeavors, Kenneth aims to inspire and connect with a diverse community of art lovers and makers.
