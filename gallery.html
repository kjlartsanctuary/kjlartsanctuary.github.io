Luggage Mixed Media Series

A vibrant celebration of color, form, and travel — this series blends acrylic and oil pastel into bold, abstract visions of luggage. Each piece bursts with personality and movement, capturing the soulful journey of stories packed and waiting to unfold.

Men’s Series

This ongoing collection honors the strength, resilience, and quiet dignity of hardworking men from all walks of life. Originally inspired by the realization that most of Kenneth J. Lewis Sr.’s early works featured women, this series shifts the focus—capturing the spirit of brotherhood and everyday greatness. With 60 pieces planned, each painting is added here as it’s completed.

Basquiat Series

Inspired by the spirit of Jean-Michel Basquiat, this series channels raw energy, graffiti aesthetics, and cultural commentary. Lewis infuses his own voice into the visual language of rebellion, symbolism, and layered storytelling.

Harlem Renaissance Series

This series pays homage to the cultural, artistic, and intellectual explosion of the Harlem Renaissance era. Each piece honors the spirit, community, and creativity that defined this historic movement.
