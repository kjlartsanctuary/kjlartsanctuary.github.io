Luggage Mixed Media Series

A vibrant celebration of color, form, and travel — this series blends acrylic and oil pastel into bold, abstract visions of luggage. Each piece bursts with personality and movement, capturing the soulful journey of stories packed and waiting to unfold.

Structure Series

A vibrant exploration of abstract form and expression—this series weaves layers of bold color, geometry, and movement into powerful figurative compositions. Each piece pulses with energy and individuality, where form is reimagined and emotion is built through color.

Where the Daisy Grows | Series

A gentle journey through whimsical landscapes—each painting invites you to search for a single daisy nestled within vibrant scenes of color and imagination. These narrative works blend charm and quiet wonder, where every daisy becomes a signature, a clue, and a quiet anchor in an unfolding story.
