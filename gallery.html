KJL Art Sanctuary Gallery

Men’s Series

This ongoing collection honors the strength, resilience, and quiet dignity of hardworking men from all walks of life. Originally inspired by the realization that most of Kenneth J. Lewis Sr.’s early works featured women, this series shifts the focus—capturing the spirit of brotherhood and everyday greatness. With 60 pieces planned, each painting is added here as it’s completed.

Basquiat Series

Inspired by the spirit of Jean-Michel Basquiat, this series channels raw energy, graffiti aesthetics, and cultural commentary. Lewis infuses his own voice into the visual language of rebellion, symbolism, and layered storytelling.

Figurative Series

A celebration of form, emotion, and identity—these figurative pieces bring characters to life with vibrant movement and narrative presence. Kenneth J. Lewis Sr. explores the human spirit through rich color and dynamic design.

Abstract Series

A collection of powerful abstract works by Kenneth J. Lewis Sr., exploring form, color, and texture across a variety of materials. These dynamic compositions invite the viewer to engage with emotion, rhythm, and energy beyond the figurative.

Ephemeral Glow Series

A symbolic series of a single burning candle and hourglass, each painted in different color palettes. These works reflect on the fleeting nature of time and light—both powerful, both passing.
