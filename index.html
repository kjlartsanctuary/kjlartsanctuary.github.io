Easter Saturday Live Painting Event

Saturday, April 4, 2026 Quaker Bridge Mall Free & Family Friendly

Join me Easter Saturday, April 4, 2026, at Quaker Bridge Mall (Center Court – 1st Floor) for a special live painting event the day before Easter. I’ll be creating a vibrant 48 x 60 painting live from open to close, and you’ll be able to watch the artwork come to life throughout the day.

This family-friendly event will also include a kids Easter card-making station, where children can create their own handmade Easter cards while experiencing art in action. Bring the family, enjoy the spirit of the Easter season, and spend the day surrounded by creativity and color.
