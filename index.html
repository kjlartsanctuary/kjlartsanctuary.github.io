Holiday Magic is happening at Quaker Bridge Mall.

Join me on Sunday, December 21 from 10 AM to 8 PM for a full day of live painting near Center Court. I’ll be creating a brand-new 48" × 60" artwork on site, and everyone is welcome to stop by, watch, and enjoy the creative process.
