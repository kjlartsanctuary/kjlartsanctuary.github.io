BOUND FOR PROMISE

Book Release & Original Exhibition

Saturday, August 15, 2026

11:00 AM – 7:00 PM

KJL Art Sanctuary Gallery & Studio
310 4th Street
Ewing, NJ 08638

Join artist Kenneth J. Lewis Sr. for the official release of BOUND FOR PROMISE, a hardcover fine art book featuring 17 original paintings and inspirational writings celebrating faith, discipline, family, education, perseverance, hope, and purpose.

For the first time, visitors will experience all 17 original oil paintings featured in the book exhibited together inside the KJL Art Sanctuary Gallery & Studio.

Collector’s Edition Hardcover — $109
  • Limited First Edition of only 150 signed and numbered hardcover books
  • Complete set of 17 collectible 5x7 prints, one of every image in the book
  • One signed 8x10 fine art print
  • First Edition Collector designation

Originally inspired by a family photograph from Harlem during the 1930s, Bound For Promise grew into a visual and written journey honoring the first seventeen years of a young man’s life — the years where faith, character, discipline, and purpose are formed.

Visitors will have the opportunity to view the complete exhibition, purchase the Collector’s Edition book, acquire original artwork, and meet the artist throughout the day.
