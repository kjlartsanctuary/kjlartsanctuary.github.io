Welcome to the official website of Artist Kenneth J. Lewis Sr.—a prolific, self-taught painter whose powerful, intuitive works span multiple styles and mediums. From abstract forms to figurative expressions, Kenneth’s work invites viewers into a world shaped by geometry, emotion, and bold experimentation. Explore a world where storytelling meets color, and every brushstroke has purpose.

Here you’ll find original artwork, behind-the-scenes studio content, podcast episodes, and announcements for upcoming exhibitions and gallery events. Step inside the sanctuary—where art lives, breathes, and speaks.
