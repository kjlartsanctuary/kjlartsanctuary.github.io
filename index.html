Welcome to the KJL Art Sanctuary

Where abstract meets spirit, and every painting tells a story.

I’m Kenneth J. Lewis Sr., a self-taught painter who didn’t pick up a brush until the age of 47. My journey is rooted in raw intuition and relentless exploration. What started as one painting turned into a lifelong passion—building entire series that speak to the soul.

I work primarily in acrylic and oil glazes on canvas, wood, and architectural panels. From abstract textures to figurative energy, my work often begins with chaos—then finds form.

This site is the home of my creative world. Feel free to browse my gallery, catch an episode of the KJL Podcast Channel, or reach out for a studio visit at the KJL Art Sanctuary in Ewing, NJ.
