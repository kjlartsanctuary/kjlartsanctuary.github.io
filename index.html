Ironing and Wash Day are two individual original paintings. Each artwork is auctioned and sold separately.

Ironing

Original painting by Kenneth J. Lewis Sr.

Size: 12 × 12 inches

Medium: Acrylic on architectural panel

Presentation: Framed

Starting bid: $150

Ironing presents an expressive figure engaged in the familiar rhythm of household work. Loose lines, layered textures, and cool blue tones suggest movement, memory, and the quiet dignity found within an everyday domestic task.

Starting Bid $150

Wash Day

Original painting by Kenneth J. Lewis Sr.

Size: 12 × 12 inches

Medium: Acrylic on architectural panel

Presentation: Framed

Starting bid: $150

Wash Day uses expressive shapes, layered marks, and varying shades of blue to suggest a figure surrounded by the activity of washing. The painting transforms an ordinary household routine into a textured reflection on labor, care, and daily life.

Starting Bid $150

Auction Rules

  1. This auction is open to the public. No membership is required to place a bid.
  2. Ironing and Wash Day are two separate auction lots. A separate bid must be submitted for each painting you wish to purchase.
  3. The starting bid for each original painting is $150. Bids below the stated starting amount will not be accepted.
  4. All bids must be submitted through the official bid form provided on this page.
  5. Bidders must provide a valid name, email address, telephone number, the painting title, and the amount of their bid.
  6. A submitted bid is considered a serious offer to purchase the selected artwork. Please do not submit a bid unless you are prepared to complete the purchase.
  7. The highest valid bid received before the auction deadline will be considered the winning bid for that painting.
  8. In the event that identical highest bids are received, the bid submitted first will receive priority.
  9. The auction closes on Wednesday, July 15, 2026 at 6:00 PM Eastern Time. Bids received after the closing time will not be considered.
  10. The winning bidder will be contacted directly using the email address or telephone number submitted with the bid.
  11. The winning bidder must complete payment within 24 hours of receiving notification. If payment is not completed, the artwork may be offered to the next highest bidder.
  12. Applicable shipping, delivery, insurance, and sales tax are additional unless otherwise stated. Local pickup may be arranged through KJL Art Sanctuary Gallery & Studio.
  13. All artwork is sold as shown and described. All completed auction sales are final.
  14. KJL Art Sanctuary and artist Kenneth J. Lewis Sr. reserve the right to reject incomplete, fraudulent, or unverifiable bids.
