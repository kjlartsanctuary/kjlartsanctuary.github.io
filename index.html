Amber Street Afternoon

36 x 44 inches · Acrylic and oil pastel on architectural panel · Part of the Architectural Daydreams series
Opening Bid: $795

Bold blocks of orange, teal, and gold form a sun washed row of buildings, while a vintage car idles in the foreground. Shadows stretch across the road, hinting at late day calm. The world here feels paused, holding its breath before the evening glow.

Constellation of Fragments

30 x 40 inches · Ink, oil pastel and acrylic on canvas
Opening Bid: $695

Layered circles, luminous color fields, and gestural marks gather like signals from different worlds meeting on a single plane. Blues, violets, and electric pinks pulse against darker passages, creating a rhythm of tension and release. The composition feels both archaeological and futuristic, as if memories, symbols, and architecture have been uncovered at once.
