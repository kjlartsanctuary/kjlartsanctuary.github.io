BOUND FOR PROMISE

Stories of Faith, Discipline, Hope, and Becoming

Extended preorder due to customer requests. The special Collector’s Edition preorder price of $79 has been extended until June 19, Juneteenth Day. After June 19, the Collector’s Edition price will increase to $109.

BOUND FOR PROMISE is a hardcover fine art book featuring 17 original paintings and inspirational writings created to encourage young men and celebrate faith, family, discipline, education, perseverance, and purpose.

The 17 images also symbolize the first 17 years in a young boy’s life — those critical years where character, values, dreams, and identity are being formed.

This First Edition Collector Release is limited to 150 signed and numbered hardcover books and includes:
  • Signed and numbered hardcover book
  • Complete set of 17 collectible 5x7 prints, one of every image in the book
  • One signed 8x10 fine art print
  • First Edition Collector designation

This project began with a single family photograph from Harlem in the 1930s and grew into a powerful visual and written journey about promise, potential, and becoming.
