About the KJL Podcast Channel

The KJL Podcast Channel is where creativity meets conversation. From behind-the-scenes moments in the studio to deep dives with featured muses, live painting sessions, and art history explorations, each episode captures the soul of the creative process. Hosted by artist Kenneth J. Lewis Sr., the channel showcases stories, people, and the transformative power of art. Whether you're an artist, collector, or curious viewer—there’s something here for you.
